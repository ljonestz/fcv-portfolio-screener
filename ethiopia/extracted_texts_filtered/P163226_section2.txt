A. Institutional and Implementation Arrangements 
56. 
The CSA will be the project-implementing agency, and is a key agency responsible 
for the overall NSDS program, along with the Ministry of Finance and Economic 
Development (MOFED) and the Statistical Council (SC). The CSA has prepared its draft 
project operational manual (OM) and project procurement plan for the next years.  
57. 
Annual Work Plan. In order to better plan and advance implementation an annual 
planning exercise is to take place as part of implementation arrangements by the CSA, the main 
implementing agency of the project. The annual work plan review will help distil lessons and 
make adjustments in keeping with regional preferences.
